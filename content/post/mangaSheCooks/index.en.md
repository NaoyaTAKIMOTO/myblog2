## Thoughts
Why do I need to be a woman? This is a story about a woman who wants to cook a lot but can't consume it all by herself.

It's a story about a woman who has to cook for the opposite sex and her family.

### The distance between two people
This is not a yuri story.

It's not a yuri story, and it's not as intimate as a girl friend story.

It's more like hobby friends.

There are problems they can share because they are the same sex.
There are some problems that we can share because we are the same sex, and there are some personalities that are different even though we are the same sex.

### The inconvenience of having a hobby in cooking
I think it's okay to have a hobby of cooking.
I also like cooking and baking.

However, when it becomes a woman's hobby, the protagonist's conflict is important because she feels depressed that people around her think it's for her family or for men.

If you love to cook, how do you find people to eat the food you cook? If you love to cook, how do you find people to eat your food?

If you have a family, there will be people who will eat the food you make for them.
However, cooking as a part of daily chores and cooking as a hobby for pleasure have different meanings.

There is also the stress of having one's feelings of enjoyment interpreted as an appeal to the opposite sex.
It is up to the other person to decide what they think.
However, it does not eliminate the feeling of resistance to not being understood in the way you want to be understood.

The solution to this resistance and discomfort is to have a neighbor who is of the same sex and who is a big eater.

If you can find someone who is willing to eat your food, it will motivate you to cook better.

### Directions.
There are times when the character is unable to cook due to health problems.
This explains the reality of having a woman as the main character.

However, this does not mean that the suffering is pushed out entirely.
But it is not a full-on depiction of suffering, but rather a depiction of what naturally happens when a woman is alive.

The moderate sense of life in the story emphasizes the realism of the main character.
